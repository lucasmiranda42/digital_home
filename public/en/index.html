Lucas Miranda

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Max Planck Institute of Biochemistry in Munich, Germany. I currently work at the Machine Learning and Systems Biology (MLSB) department, directed by Karsten Borgwardt. Previously, I obtained my PhD in 2024 from the Technical University of Munich (TUM), under the supervision of Bertram Müller Myhsok. My research focuses on the development of machine learning methods for biochemical and medical informatics problems.
